🔐 Token Encryptor

Run this once on your PC. Enter your Dropbox token and choose a password. Copy the encrypted output into index.html.

How it works: Your token is encrypted with RC4 using a key derived from your password + a random salt (10,000 key-stretching rounds). The encrypted string is safe to hardcode in your app's source code. Only your password can decrypt it.
